Starting A New City

You can start (settle) a new city in several ways (assuming that a the active unit is capable of doing so):

  • Click Actions on the toolbar. Choose Settle a new city... from the dropdown menu.
  • Press the 'B' button when your settler is active.
  • Right click on your active settler and choose Settle a new city... from the popup menu.

Once you have

chosen to settle a new city, a popup window will ask you to name the city. Type the name of the new city in the window and press OK.
